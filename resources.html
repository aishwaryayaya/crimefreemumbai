Cyber Security tips

  • Keep antivirus and operating system up to date in you devices.
  • Regularly backup your sensitive/important data at regular intervals.
  • Be careful while opening suspicious web links/URLs.
  • Keep your password/PIN confidential.
  • Use strong passwords with a combination of special characters, numbers, upper case and lower case.
  • Keep changing your passwords periodically and do not use the same password in multiple accounts.
  • Use the latest security tools like firewall, anti-virus software to protect your critical data and privacy.

Frequently asked Q/A

What is the purpose of Cyber Crime Reporting Portal?

This portal is an initiative to facilitate victims/ complainants to report cyber crime complaints online. This portal caters all types of cyber crime complaints including complaints pertaining to online Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) or sexually explicit content such as Rape/Gang Rape (CP/RGR) content and other cyber crimes such as mobile crimes, online and social media crimes, online financial frauds, hacking, cryptocurrency crimes, online cyber trafficking, Elderly complaint, Eve teasing and Domestic violence.

What is CSAM?

Child Sexually Abusive Material (CSAM) refers to material containing sexual image in any form, of a child who is abused or sexually exploited. Section 67 (B) of IT Act states that “it is punishable for publishing or transmitting of material depicting children in sexually explicit act, etc. in electronic form”.

Which type of cybercrimes I can report on the portal?

One can report complaints pertaining to online Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) or sexually explicit content such as Rape/Gang Rape (CP/RGR) content and other cyber crimes such as mobile crimes, online and social media crimes, online financial frauds, hacking, cryptocurrency crimes, online cyber trafficking, Elderly complaint, Eve teasing and Domestic violence.

What kind of information, should I provide to report complaint?

Initially, one needs to register to use the website using name,username and create a password. If one has already registered then just login using your username and password for the website.
To file a complaint first select the date when you are filling the complaint. Then add your first name, last name and enter your Email ID. Next step is to add your address,city region,pin code and name of the police station near your house. Next add the date and location of the reported incident and fill in the complaint details.
Finally submit the form and you will be reverted about your complaint in 24 hours. If the complaint is a urgent one you will be reverted by the associated authority in some time.

What type of information would be considered as evidence while filing my complaint related to cybercrime?

It is important to keep any evidence you may have related to your complaint. Evidence may include:

  • Credit card receipt
  • Bank statement
  • Envelope (if received a letter or item through mail or courier)
  • Brochure/Pamphlet
  • Online money transfer receipt
  • Copy of email
  • URL of webpage
  • Chat transcripts
  • Suspect mobile number screenshot
  • Videos
  • Images
  • Any other kind of document

What happens once I report a complaint?

The complaints reported on the portal shall be handled by the concerned State/UT police authorities based on your selection of State/ UT while reporting the complaint.

Can I withdraw my complaint from the portal?

A complaint once filed under any section cannot be deleted.
